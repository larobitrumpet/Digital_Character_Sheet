Create Your Stats.

Roll 4d6 and take out the lowest number. Add up the remaining numbers and put the result in a box. Do this 5 more time to fill in all the boxes. If you don't like your stats, you can throw them all away and get completely new ones, but you can only do this three times. You can also press "Generate" to automaticly generate stats. You can also scroll down for alternative ways of generating stats.
